The dataset consists of stock price details of Google over 5 years- 2012-2016

Atttributes:
1.Date
2.Open -  opening stock price
3.High - peak stock price
4.Low - bottom stock price
5.Close - closing stock price
6.Volume

A Time Series analysis model has to be trained on this data to learn the variations in trends.

It is then to be tested to predicted the 'Open' stock price trend for the month of January in 2017.
