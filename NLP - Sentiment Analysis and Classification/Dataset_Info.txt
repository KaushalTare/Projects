The dataset is a '.tsv' file containing the feedback from the customers of a restaurant.

Attributes:

"Feedback Text"	<< Tab >?	Implication/Conclusion

The implication of feedback is given by-
 
0 - not liked/negative review
1 - liked/positive review

Model needs to learn, predict and classify the reviews into positive and negative,
to analyze the functioning of restaurant and thus help in making useful business decisions.
