Geodemographic Segmentation Model:  Predicting the Customer Churn Rate for a Bank

The dataset consists of various customer related features collected by the bank over certain period of time.
The features are as follows:
1. Row Number
2. Customer ID
3. Surname
4. Credit Score
5. Geography
6. Gender
7. Age
8. Tenure
9. Balance
10. Number of Products
11. Has Credit Card
12. Is Active Member
13. Estimated Salary
14. Exited

Task: To predict the binary variable (customer exits? -Yes/No) based on the correlations between different features of a customer.
